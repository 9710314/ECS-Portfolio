Spotlight: Museums

My interest in dinosuars really started in the museums I went to as a kid. There I learned about different species and timelines, but dinosuars really stuck out to me. After that, I continued research and my interest grew.

Key Insight

I really like researching the different kinds of dinosuars and the differences of each. Especially how carnivores and herbivores interact and the balance between them.

Who, What, When, Where, How?

Georges Cuvier or "the Father of Paleontology created the Comparative Method of Organismal Biology. Which compaes the characteristics of different species to understand how traits evolved over time, a revolutionary idea.
